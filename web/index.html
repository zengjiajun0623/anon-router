Trust-minimized lane coming soon

The lane above is custodial: we hold your float. This lane will not. Your deposit sits in an escrow contract we cannot touch, and you can reclaim it unilaterally at any time. A stale or rolled-back close is provably challengeable.

The protocol runs today from the CLI against a local testnet (cli.py chat --channel). In-browser proving is not finished, so this lane is switched off on the hosted demo. No buttons here until they do something real.
