What's private

No signup, no card, no KYC✓ (a deposit makes a pseudonymous account; a voucher makes none)
No direct link from a spend to your deposit✓ blind-signed ecash
The provider only ever sees the router, never you✓
Honest limits: the blind signatures remove the direct cryptographic link, but in practice unlinkability is only as strong as how many others are claiming and spending in the same window (small on this early alpha). The router still sees amounts and timing, and our app doesn't log IPs but the hosting edge can, so connect over the .onion and space out claims and spends. The model reads your prompt (use a local/* model to keep it off third parties); the balance is custodial. Full posture at /privacy.
Testnet demo. Deposits use Sepolia test ETH; no real funds are involved. Today's deposits sit in a custodial vault (we hold the float — keep balances small). The non-custodial escrow, whose safety core is machine-checked (Lean), is on the roadmap.
